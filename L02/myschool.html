St Charles de Foucauld (SDCF) is a private catholic school. It has twelve years of experience and is for classes from 6ème to 3ème.

The school provides many subjects which are : french, english, spanish, maths, physics, chemistry, history, geography, sciences, pe, and cathcism. It also has latin and grecque as options.

It is a mix school where all religions, cultures and race are accepted. Boys and girls are in the same classrooms but during break, girls have access to the gardens and boys must stay on the football or basketball court.

SCDF has a many teachers, all with diplomas, who already have many years of experience and are from the best schools in Europe.

The school probably seems very strict but it is all for the students safety as long as for the teachers.

We also have a uniform which is white for the top which must absolutely cover the shoulders and dark blue for the trousers for boys and skirts for girls ( the skirts mustn't be above the knees.

SCDF also has a favorite quotation which is :

"Travaillez bien, étudiez bien, tout à Jésus"

Which means "work well, study well, all for Jesus"
