Flowers:

Flowers are beautiful and colorful.
They grow in gardens, parks, and forests. Flowers come in many shapes and sizes.
Some are big, and some are small. Flowers can be red, yellow, pink, or purple.
Bees and butterflies love flowers because they make honey and help plants grow.
Flowers also have a nice smell.
People give flowers to show love or celebrate special days.
A flower is not just pretty; it also helps the environment.
Flowers give us oxygen and help make the world a better place.
